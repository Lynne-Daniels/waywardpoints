Map of BRAG 2008

The 29th Annual Bike Ride Across Georgia

Details of the ride available here: www.brag.org

The data for this map is from www.georgiatandemrally.com/braggps.html. This impressive collection of tracks and waypoints was assembled and marked up by Roger Strauss. Kudos and accolades are due to the cartographer.
