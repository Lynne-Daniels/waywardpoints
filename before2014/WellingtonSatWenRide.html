Wellington Monday and Wednesday Bike Ride


A small group of riders meets in front of Bageland in the Publix shopping Plaza on Forest Hill in Wellington, FL at 8AM on Saturdays and Wednesdays. Speed is moderate, riders are a mix of road, touring and recumbent riders. This is a welcoming group of interesting well-travelled people, and there is usually breakfast afterwards. Some variations are usually added to the route by smaller groups of riders.
