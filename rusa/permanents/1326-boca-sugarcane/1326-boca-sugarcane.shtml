Ride Description

This ride begins in suburban Boca Raton and passes through Wellington before making a big loop through sugarcane, agricultural, and ranching areas. Roads vary from busy thoroughfares to county highways, all with decent shoulders/bike lanes. The western sections of the ride have few services. In hot weather, be sure to carry sufficient water out there.
